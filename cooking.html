Program Overview

Our 6-week Cooking Life Skills Training Program is designed to teach essential culinary skills while promoting healthier eating habits and practical cooking techniques. Perfect for beginners and those looking to refine their kitchen skills, this program combines hands-on cooking experience with valuable life skills.

Course Highlights:

  1. Week 1: Kitchen Fundamentals and Basic Cooking Techniques
    • Overview: Get started with the basics of kitchen organization and fundamental cooking techniques.
    • Course Highlights:
      • Overview of kitchen tools and equipment: knives, pots, pans, and utensils.
      • Basic knife skills: chopping, slicing, and dicing.
      • Cooking techniques: saut√©ing, boiling, and baking.
      • Safety and hygiene practices: food storage and handling.
    • Hands-On Project: Prepare a simple dish using basic techniques, such as a vegetable stir-fry or pasta.
    • Who Should Enroll: Beginners who want to build a solid foundation in cooking and kitchen management.
  2. Week 2: Meal Planning and Budgeting
    • Overview: Learn how to plan nutritious and budget-friendly meals for various needs and preferences.
    • Course Highlights:
      • Principles of meal planning: creating balanced menus and grocery lists.
      • Budgeting for groceries: understanding cost-effective shopping and reducing food waste.
      • Techniques for batch cooking and meal prep.
      • Exploring different dietary needs and preferences.
    • Hands-On Project: Create a weekly meal plan and cook a meal using budget-friendly ingredients.
    • Who Should Enroll: Individuals looking to manage their food budget effectively while planning nutritious meals.
  3. Week 3: Cooking Techniques and Recipe Development
    • Overview: Enhance your cooking skills by mastering a range of techniques and experimenting with recipe creation.
    • Course Highlights:
      • Advanced cooking techniques: roasting, grilling, and poaching.
      • Understanding flavor profiles and seasoning.
      • Basics of recipe development and modification.
      • Tips for cooking various proteins and vegetables.
    • Hands-On Project: Develop and cook a recipe, experimenting with different ingredients and techniques.
    • Who Should Enroll: Those looking to expand their culinary repertoire and gain confidence in creating and modifying recipes.
  4. Week 4: Healthy Cooking and Nutrition
    • Overview: Focus on cooking techniques that promote health and wellness, with an emphasis on nutrition.
    • Course Highlights:
      • Basics of nutrition: understanding macronutrients and micronutrients.
      • Techniques for preparing healthy meals: reducing fat, sugar, and sodium.
      • Incorporating fruits, vegetables, and whole grains into your diet.
      • Cooking methods that preserve nutritional value.
    • Hands-On Project: Prepare a nutritious meal with a focus on healthy ingredients and balanced nutrition.
    • Who Should Enroll: Individuals interested in improving their diet and cooking healthier meals.
  5. Week 5: International Cuisine and Cultural Exploration
    • Overview: Explore global flavors and cooking styles by preparing dishes from different cultures.
    • Course Highlights:
      • Introduction to various international cuisines: Italian, Mexican, Asian, and more.
      • Techniques and ingredients unique to different cultures.
      • Understanding cultural significance and traditional cooking methods.
      • Tips for adapting international recipes to local ingredients.
    • Hands-On Project: Cook a dish from a selected international cuisine and explore cultural culinary traditions.
    • Who Should Enroll: Food enthusiasts and those interested in expanding their culinary horizons with global recipes.
  6. Week 6: Cooking for Special Occasions and Presentation Skills
    • Overview: Learn how to prepare and present meals for special occasions, including festive and formal events.
    • Course Highlights:
      • Techniques for creating impressive dishes for celebrations and gatherings.
      • Presentation skills: plating, garnishing, and creating visually appealing dishes.
      • Planning and preparing multi-course meals.
      • Tips for hosting and entertaining guests.
    • Hands-On Project: Plan, prepare, and present a multi-course meal suitable for a special occasion or dinner party.
    • Who Should Enroll: Anyone looking to enhance their cooking skills for entertaining and special events.
